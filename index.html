The Malaysia Nursing Council will be hosting our annual nursing conference this year, "Malaysia Nursing Conference 2020" on DECEMBER 4th-6th, 2020 @ Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre.



The online registration system is now open! We cordially invite you to grab this great opportunity to share, to exchange and to learn with colleagues from around the world.




 


 


 


 

Malaysia Nursing Conference 2020 provide golden opportunities to guide career in nursing, where one can learn new strategies, work with new technologies and gain ideas from experts at the forepart of medical and healthcare. This event also provide networking opportunities to connect with experts, meet and greet sessions and many more to expand their network horizons. This conferece gather thousands of nurses explored the many ways in which nurses work to achieve universal access to health, not only providing health care but also addressing the social determinants of health, such as education, gender equality, poverty, etc.

 

 

Who should participate ?

The annual conference is intended for nurses in the healthcare and medical industry where professionals throughout the Malaysia health ecosystem connect for education, innovation and networking.

 

This Year's Edition:

The theme for our conference is “Leading innovation in nursing” with interesting and latest conference topics that will instantly pique the interests of participants, also for educational research directed towards innovation.

 

Who will attend ?

The conference anticipate more than 20 speakers, which enables participants to learn from prominent industry experts, university professors, clinical researchers and many more invited from all over the world to share their investigation and experiences on all parts of the rapidly and evolving industry of healthcare, also demonstrating latest healthcare techniques.

Dear colleagues and friends, It gives us great pleasure to welcome you to the Malaysia Nursing Conference 2020! The professional roles of nurses have evolved and expanded significantly over the past decade, spanning from acute to chronic settings and ranging from community to global health as well as in the realm of research, education and innovation. The shift in roles of nurses beyond bedside encourages our nurses to continuously improve and incorporate evidence-based practices into patient care in tandem with the ever-changing healthcare landscape. This year’s conference, with the theme “Leading innovation in nursing”, aims to showcase the advancement in nursing research, education and clinical innovation in our efforts to reinvent nursing practices.

 

 

 

Featuring a strong line-up of inspiring speakers from healthcare and other industries, the Conference programme will include a myriad of topics on innovation, advanced skills acquisition and nurse-led translational work to inspire greater development in the care of our patients. At the same time, the Conference provides an opportunity for us to reflect and restructure our practices to align with the changing healthcare climate. You can expect a series of lectures on key topics that shape the future of nursing practice such as transformation in leadership, advanced nursing implementations, competency building, assistive robotics in healthcare as well as information and communications technology. On behalf of the organising committee, we look forward to welcoming you to this hallmark event where you can forge collaborations with like-minded nursing professionals and learn from one another to deliver better care. We wish you a fruitful learning experience as you make new friends, reconnect with old ones and continue to ignite ideas together to reinvent nursing for the future.
